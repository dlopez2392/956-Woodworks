The maker

“I watched a video and said — I can do that.”

And I did. What started as curiosity became a craft. I spend most of my free time learning to plan, design, and execute pieces that push me to a new level of craftsmanship — and each one is usually one of a kind.

Modern and farmhouse furniture, furniture restoration, woodturned bowls, and handmade kitchen goods — built for people who appreciate quality that lasts for years.
